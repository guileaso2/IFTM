BULLY

by Kanye West

ABOUT THE ALBUM


BULLY is the twelfth studio album by Ye, formerly known as Kanye West. The project presents a darker and more experimental atmosphere, mixing industrial rap, distorted sounds, emotional lyrics and cinematic production.


The album explores themes such as pressure, fame, isolation, family, identity and personal struggles. Throughout the project, Ye combines aggressive instrumentals with calmer and emotional moments, creating a contrast between chaos and vulnerability.


Visually, BULLY follows a minimalist and dystopian aesthetic inspired by underground fashion, black-and-white photography and experimental art. The album also continues Ye's tradition of reinventing his sound and pushing creative boundaries with every new era.


Fans describe the project as a mix between the raw energy of Yeezus, the emotional tone of 808s & Heartbreak, and the modern experimental style seen in his more recent works.
